Why Join Us

  • Amity Business School Accredited by Accreditation Council for Business School and Program, ACBSP, USA
  • Audiology & Speech Language Pathology programs accredited by Rehabilitation Council of India and Amity School of Engineering & Technology accredited by IET, UK
  • Promotes various research activities among students and Amity University Gurgaon has multiple Center of Excellence such as Centre for Financial Analytics, Centre for BRICS Studies and many more
  • Promotes entrepreneurship through Amity Innovation Incubator and since its inception more than 100 has been incubated through this
  • Amity has tie-ups with over 100 international universities, research centers in areas of student exchange, faculty exchange, joint research, curriculum development and educational tours for students
  • The university follows Choice Base Credit System (CBCS) and conducts regular industry interaction to minimize the gap between industry expectation and university delivery
