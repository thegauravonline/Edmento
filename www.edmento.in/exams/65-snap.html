About SNAP 2022

Symbiosis National Aptitude Test is an entrance examination for admission to the MBA programmes at Symbiosis Institutes located in numerous parts of India. Those who are looking forward to giving an examination in 2020 need to qualify the examination. The announcement of the Symbiosis International University or SIU Pune will be made in August 2020, thus the eligible candidates need to register shortly. Before making the preparation of the exam and applying for it, make sure for fetching details like SNAP 2020-21 Application Form Date, Exam Pattern, Eligibility, Admit Card and Syllabus, etc.

Want to know which colleges you can apply to with the SNAP score ?

Process

The merit list will be based on the given below parameters:

  • SNAP Score from 180 scales down to 50 50
  • Group Exercise 10
  • Personal Interaction 30
  • Written Ability Test 10


Eligibility

  • Candidates required having the nationality of India, Overseas Citizen of Indian Foreign national, Person of Indian Origin, or Non-Residential India
  • Those who have completed graduation with at least 50% marks from the recognized university can apply for the exam. However, for SC and ST category students, the percentage will be 40%. Graduation should do from the recognized university. In case, if the candidate has a foreign degree then he/she must be having the certificate from the Association of Indian Universities (AIU)

Syllabus

The SNAP Syllabus 2020 is divided into four different sections which are as below:

  • General Awareness: Social issues, Sports, Important quotations, Finance, Entertainment, Automobiles, World Records, History, Books and authors, Politics Major corporate events Famous award and prizes, Science, Business, Current Affairs, Geography, Top officials of big companies, International organizations, Punch line of companies, etc.
  • General English: Sentence Correction, Sentence correction, Comprehension of passage (Poems can also come), Syllogisms, Foreign language words used in English, Contextual usage, Jumbled paragraphs with four or five sentences, Sentence completion, Fill In the Blanks, Verbal Reasoning, Jumbled paragraphs (6 sentences with first and last fixed), Different usage of same word, Antonyms, Analogies, Odd one out, Idioms, One word substitution, etc.
  • Quantitative Ability: Pipes and Cisterns, Allegation & Mixtures, Simple Interest & Compound Interest, Work, Set Theory, Probability, Clocks, Vectors, Installment Payments, Trigonometry, Co-ordinate geometry, Permutations & Combinations, Logarithm, Ratios and Proportion, Partnership, Calendar, Geometry, In-equations Quadratic and linear equations Algebra, Time-Speed-Distance, Number system, HCF & LCM, Work and time, Binomial Expansion, Venn Diagram, Profit & Loss, Averages, Geometric Progression, Median, Mode, Mensuration, Number Base System, Geometric mean, Harmonic mean, Arithmetic progression, Arithmetic mean, Maxima & Minima Progression, Percentages, BODMAS, Surds & Indices, Complex numbers, etc.
  • Reasoning: Probably false, definitely true, Functions, Syllogisms, Symbol Based problems, Family tree identifying the relationship among the group of people, Linear arrangements, definitely false kind of statement, Coding and decoding, Matrix arrangements, Sequencing, Graphs can be Column graphs, Data Interpretation based on text, Bar Graphs, Line charts, Graphs representing Area, Venn diagram, Pie Chart, Identifying next number in series, Critical reasoning, graphs & tables, Assumption-Premise-Conclusion, Visual reasoning,  Identifying valid inferences, Identifying Strong arguments and Weak arguments, Puzzles, Assertion and reasons, Statements and assumptions, Cause and Effect, Identifying Probably true, Statements and conclusions, etc.

Pattern

  • Question Type: Each question have 4 options
  • Duration: Total duration will be  2 Hours
  • Marking Scheme: Each question will be 1 Marks
  • Negative Marking:  25% Negative marking for wrong answer
